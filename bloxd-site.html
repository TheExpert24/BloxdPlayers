The Bloxd Players is a Stanford Online High School Society that was founded to have fun playing bloxd.io and to build a public server in honor of those who perished on September 11, 2001. Our mission is to remember those who perished and were affected by the 9/11 attacks as well as an OHS student that died in 2025.
We meet every Friday at 5-6 PM PST to work on the server while collaborating skillfully. Everyone is welcome as long as you follow the terms of service listed below and play respectfully and carefully.
